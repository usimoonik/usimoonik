## 1. Project Overview & Public Purpose

USI Moonik Token (symbol: USI) is a fixed-supply BEP-20 token deployed on the  
BNB Smart Chain.

The project was created exclusively as an educational and transparency-focused  
blockchain reference. It demonstrates how a simple, immutable smart contract can  
operate publicly without hidden logic or financial incentives.

The token is **NOT** designed as a commercial or investment product.  
Its purpose is limited to learning, experimentation, and on-chain verification.

**Total Supply:** 999,000,000,000 USI (permanently fixed)  
**Decimals:** 18  
**Network:** BNB Smart Chain  
**Contract:** Fully verified on BscScan  

The entire supply was minted only once at deployment and cannot be increased  
or modified.

## 3. Smart Contract Transparency

The smart contract was intentionally designed to remain minimal,  
predictable, and easily auditable.

The contract explicitly excludes:

- Minting functionality  
- Burning functionality  
- Transaction taxes or fees  
- Blacklist or freeze controls  
- Administrative balance manipulation  
- Upgradeable proxy logic  

All behavior is permanently defined by the deployed code and publicly verifiable  
on BscScan. No hidden or privileged mechanisms exist.

About USI Moonik

USI Moonik Token (USI) is a BEP-20 utility token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain with a permanently fixed total supply of 999,000,000,000 USI.

The project focuses on transparency, simplicity, and publicly verifiable smart contract behavior. All core information is openly documented and accessible. The smart contract was fully verified on BscScan as of 2025-12-04.

USI Moonik was not created for fundraising, investment, or speculative purposes. There was no ICO, IEO, IDO, private sale, or public sale. This token is designed purely for educational purposes, demonstrating blockchain utility, and promoting transparent on-chain interactions without any promises of financial gain or profit.

The term “Moonik” refers solely to the project's name and does not imply any financial growth, "moonshot" speculation, or guarantees of value appreciation. It emphasizes transparency, verifiability, and open documentation. It does not imply custodial services, fiduciary responsibility, or guarantees of any kind.

USI Moonik is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Binance, Trust Wallet, or any third-party organization.

The project is maintained by an identifiable founder with public professional profiles, ensuring accountability and compliance with transparency standards. The core team includes dedicated members who contribute to development and community support.
